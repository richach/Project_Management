Career Paths

Career Opportunities

- Associate Product Manager

Google INC

Partner with engineering, product, marketing, business development, and other teams within Google to expand Fuchsia’s usage on devices. Understand the industry landscape, products, and customer needs to evolve strategic plans. Be the expert in metrics and data for the product, understanding and ensuring a data-backed approach to product quality. Engage at all parts of a product lifecycle as the representative of the user.

- Product Manager

MongoDB

Defines the product vision, strategy, and roadmap. Gathers, manages, and prioritizes market/customer requirements. Acts as the customer advocate articulating the user’s and/or buyer’s needs. Works closely with engineering, sales, marketing, and support to ensure business case and customer satisfaction goals are met.

- Senior Product Manager

Microsoft CRP

A Microsoft PM is someone who develops, advocates for, and advances the company's product vision. Also, they create the product roadmap, work with the customers to get their feedback, and operate in a cross-functional capacity with the support, marketing, design, and engineering team. Due to the scope of the large company, Microsoft PMs directly help develop products for a variety of end-users in multiple applications and markets.

  • Product Manager, Fuchsia Devices
  • Lead Product Manager, Firebase Build, Core Developer
  • Senior Product Manager, Wear OS, Embedded SIM
